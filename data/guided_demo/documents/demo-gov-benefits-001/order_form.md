# Pricing and Order Requirements

Document: Pricing Instructions and Order Requirements
Agency: State Department of Human Services
Opportunity: Benefits Portal Modernization

## Commercial overview

The estimated contract value is $4.8M over the initial term. The agency expects
pricing to separate implementation fees, software support, optional support
tiers, and post-launch optimization services.

The response requires a cost proposal must include fixed implementation fees, optional support tiers, and service credits for missed production milestones or critical support response targets.

## Pricing notes

The account team expects margin pressure because the agency requested fixed-fee
implementation pricing while leaving some integration details unresolved.
Finance should validate whether the service-credit exposure can be accepted
without eroding target margin.

## Finance review request

Finance should build a preliminary cost model, estimate staffing cost against the
compressed timeline, and identify any pricing conditions BD/Ops should include
before the final bid/no-bid meeting.
