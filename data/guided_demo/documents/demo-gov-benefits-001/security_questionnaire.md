# Security and Privacy Questionnaire

Document: Security Questionnaire Extract
Agency: State Department of Human Services

## Data classification

The portal will process regulated citizen benefits data including eligibility records and household income. The system may also store case status, appointment history, user-submitted documents, and contact information.

## Privacy artifacts

The agency notes that a data processing addendum is required but not attached to
the RFP packet. The agency also asks the vendor to describe subcontractor access,
data-retention controls, and incident notification timelines.

## Residency and access

The questionnaire asks whether production data can remain in the United States
and whether support access can be limited to approved personnel. The final data
residency answer is not included in the packet.

## Security review request

Security should determine whether regulated data handling is supportable and
what conditions must be resolved before the team commits to the proposal.
