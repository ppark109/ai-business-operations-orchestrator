# Draft Contract Terms

Document: State Benefits Portal Modernization - Draft Terms
Agency: State Department of Human Services
Prepared for: Vendor qualification review

## Scope and obligations

The vendor will provide technical modernization support for the benefits portal,
including configuration, integration, testing, launch support, and knowledge
transfer to agency staff.

The contract requires the vendor to support audit requests related to system
availability, incident response, subcontractor access, and data-handling controls.
The agency may request reasonable audit evidence during implementation and for
one year after production launch.

## Liability and indemnity

The draft terms state that the liability cap equal to three times annual fees
will apply to claims arising from implementation delays, data-processing errors,
and service-credit disputes.

The draft terms also state that the vendor will indemnify the agency for third-party claims arising from portal operations, including claims tied to system availability, data-processing mistakes, or subcontractor access.

## Insurance

The vendor must maintain cyber liability, technology errors and omissions, and
commercial general liability insurance. Certificates of insurance must be
available before contract execution.

## Legal review request

Legal should determine whether the liability cap, indemnity wording, insurance
requirements, and audit-right language are acceptable for qualification or must
be treated as negotiation conditions before final bid authorization.
