# Draft Master Services Agreement - Everline Robotics

## 1. Services

Vendor will provide the subscription platform and standard implementation
support described in the order form. No custom source-code deliverables are
included in this draft.

## 2. Liability

The customer redline states that the aggregate liability cap is above 1x fees
paid under the order form. The proposed cap would apply to all claims except
payment obligations and confidentiality breaches.

## 3. Indemnity

The customer also inserted unusual indemnity language requiring vendor to defend
claims arising from customer-configured workflows. This is broader than the
standard indemnity position and should be reviewed before signature.

## 4. Termination

Termination for convenience remains standard. Renewal and notice periods are not
changed by this redline.
