# Order Form - Cobalt Telecom

## Subscription

The customer is purchasing the enterprise tier for a twelve month term.

## Pricing Exception

The order requests a 45% discount from list price. The discount is above the
standard approval threshold and must be reviewed by finance before booking.

## Service Credits

The order also grants 2 months SLA credits if launch support milestones are not
met. These custom credits are not part of the standard order form template.
